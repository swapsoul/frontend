SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY

We partner with Delhivery and offer free shipping across India on all orders. In case a pin code is not serviced by Delhivery, the order would be shipped through another courier.

Orders received by 2 pm are usually dispatched on the same day or latest by next business day.

Once the orders are dispatched, it usually takes 2-5 days for delivery. However, actual delivery periods may vary depending on extraneous factors.
